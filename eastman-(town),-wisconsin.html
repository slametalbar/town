Eastman (town), Wisconsin

Eastman is a town in Crawford County, Wisconsin, United States. The population was 790 at the 2000 census. The village of Eastman and the unincorporated community of Charme are located within the town.





== Geography ==

According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 72.6 square miles (188.0 km2), of which, 71.5 square miles (185.1 km2) of it is land and 1.1 square miles (3.0 km2) of it (1.57%) is water.





== Demographics ==

As of the census of 2000, there were 790 people, 287 households, and 225 families residing in the town. The population density was 11.1 people per square mile (4.3/km2). There were 475 housing units at an average density of 6.6 per square mile (2.6/km2). The racial makeup of the town was 99.37% White, 0.13% Black or African American, 0.13% Native American, and 0.38% from two or more races. 0.25% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race.

There were 287 households, out of which 35.2% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 69.0% were married couples living together, 5.2% had a female householder with no husband present, and 21.3% were non-families.
