Oconto Falls (town), Wisconsin

Oconto Falls is a town in Oconto County, Wisconsin, United States. The population was 1,139 at the 2000 census. The City of Oconto Falls is located within the town.





== Geography ==

According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 32.8 square miles (84.9 km2), of which, 32.2 square miles (83.5 km2) of it is land and 0.5 square miles (1.4 km2) of it (1.62%) is water.





== Demographics ==

As of the census of 2000, there were 1,139 people, 434 households, and 329 families residing in the town. The population density was 35.3 people per square mile (13.6/km2). There were 484 housing units at an average density of 15.0 per square mile (5.8/km2). The racial makeup of the town was 99.30% White, 0.09% Black or African American, 0.18% Native American, and 0.44% from two or more races. 0.18% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race.

There were 434 households, out of which 34.1% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 66.6% were married couples living together, 5.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 24.0% were non-families.
