Friendship (town), Wisconsin

Friendship is a town in Fond du Lac County, Wisconsin, United States. The population was 2,406 at the 2000 census. The unincorporated communities of Dexter and Van Dyne are located within the town.





== Geography ==

According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 34.4 square miles (89.2 km2), of which, 17.0 square miles (44.1 km2) of it is land and 17.4 square miles (45.1 km2) of it (50.55%) is water.





== Demographics ==

At the 2000 United States census there were 2,406 people, 971 households, and 690 families living in the town. The population density was 141.3 people per square mile (54.6/km2). There were 1,062 housing units at an average density of 62.4 per square mile (24.1/km2). The racial makeup of the town was 98.55% White, 0.21% African American, 0.17% Native American, 0.08% Asian, 0.25% Pacific Islander, 0.33% from other races, and 0.42% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 1.79%.

Of the 971 households 30.4% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 59.7% were married couples living together, 7.0% had a female householder with no husband present, and 28.9% were non-families.
